Employment & Internship Opportunities


The Maryland WIC Program is looking for qualified professional and paraprofessional staff to fill a variety of positions. Individuals seeking employment should have an interest in nutrition and breastfeeding and enjoy working with children.

Temporary Staff Full or part-time temporary workers are hired, trained and assigned to Local Agencies who are looking for short/long-term workers. Positions are state contractual with the potential for permanent employment. The positions for recruitment are: WIC Service Associate and WIC Nutrition Program Trainee. There aren't any benefits nor guaranteed a specific number of hours. Must have reliable transportation. All job training is provided.
Local Agency The Maryland WIC Program has 18 Local Agencies across the state of Maryland. Job opportunities within the Local Agency are Clerk, WIC Service Associate, Nutritionist, and Breastfeeding Peer Counsellor.
